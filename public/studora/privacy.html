3

AI Features & Third-Party Services (Groq)

Studora AI's intelligent features (study plan generation, mock tests, AI chat, voice input, photo OCR, answer evaluation) are powered by Groq Cloud — a third-party AI inference platform.

  • 🔑 AI features require you to supply your own free Groq API key, obtained directly from console.groq.com/keys. We never request, handle, or store your key on our servers.
  • 📤 When an AI feature is used, the relevant content (chapter text, your typed question, audio, or image) is sent to Groq's API servers via an HTTPS call authenticated by your key.
  • 📜 Your data sent to Groq is governed by Groq's Privacy Policy and their Terms of Service. Groq's free tier does not use prompt data to train models (as of 2025).
  • 🚫 AI features are entirely optional. If you choose not to configure a Groq key, the app works fully in offline / sample-data mode with no data leaving your device.
4

App Permissions

Studora AI requests the following device permissions. All are optional and only used for the features described:

  • 🎙️ Microphone — Used only when you initiate voice input to create tasks or timetable entries via speech. Audio is transcribed by Groq and not stored locally or by us.
  • 📷 Camera / Photo Library — Used only when you choose to photograph a chapter page for OCR text extraction. The photo is sent to Groq Vision and the temporary file is deleted from your device after processing.
  • 🔔 Notifications — Used to send local study reminders at times you configure. We do not use remote push notification infrastructure; all notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

You can revoke any permission at any time in your device's Settings. Revoking a permission only disables the corresponding optional feature — the core app continues to work.

Data Retention & Deletion

Because all data resides on your device, you are in full control:

  • 🗑️ Delete specific data — Subjects, tasks, timetable entries, and exams can be deleted individually from within the app at any time.
  • 🔄 Reset all data — Settings → About → Reset App Data wipes every piece of data stored on your device for this app.
  • 🗑️ Uninstall — Uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data (subject to OS backup settings — see below).
  • ☁️ iCloud / Google Play Backup — Your device OS may back up app data to your personal cloud account (controlled by you in device settings). We cannot access this backup.
  • 🔑 Groq API Key — Removed from your device's secure storage via Settings → Cloud AI → Remove Key at any time.
ℹ️ We hold no copy of your data on any server, so we have no data to delete from our end. Your right to erasure is satisfied by the in-app reset or uninstall.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Studora AI is designed to be used by students of all ages, including children under 13, under the supervision and with the consent of a parent or guardian.

  • 🚫 We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information (PII) from any user, including children. The name and avatar entered during onboarding are stored locally on the device and never transmitted to us.
  • 🤖 AI features (which send chapter content to Groq) are optional. Parents should review whether their child should configure a Groq API key and use AI features, as this involves sending academic content to Groq's servers.
  • 🚫 The app contains no advertising networks, social-media SDKs, or third-party analytics that are known to track children.
  • 📧 If you are a parent and have concerns, contact us at privacy@amitupadhyay.com.
7

Security

  • 🔐 Your Groq API key is stored using Expo SecureStore, which maps to iOS Keychain (AES-256 encrypted) and Android Keystore (hardware-backed on supported devices).
  • 🌐 All network calls to Groq are made over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • 📦 General app data (subjects, tasks, timetable) is stored in AsyncStorage / SQLite on your device — encrypted at rest by the device's full-disk encryption if enabled.
  • 🔑 The app does not transmit your Groq API key to any URL other than api.groq.com.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or California, you have rights including:
